RULES !

1.A team can have a maximum of two members

2. Prelims may be conducted based on technical questions.

3. Team members will undergo a minimum of 4 tasks from given no of tasks varying according to difficulty level

4. Facilities like wifi laptop will be provided to candidates

5. Malpractices committed and caught will be subjected to revocation of teams right to participate

6. Use of mobiles is strictly prohibited

7. A volunteer will be allotted exclusive to each team

8. Realtime scores of each team will be updated after each task and be displayed to the candidates

9. Evaluation of each task is done by software and Committee does not hold any responsibility for it

10. The decision made by the comittee is final and no further argument is encouraged
